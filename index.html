I help indie developers make money from their apps.

You built something people use. But you're not making money from it — maybe you don't have time, maybe you don't know how, maybe it just feels weird. I can help.

I'm Ofek. I work on Instagram at Meta during the day. Before that, I spent years at Unity helping apps monetize. Now I'm looking for a few indie developers to partner with.

How it works

Simple revenue share. I do the monetization work — premium tiers, pricing, implementation. You keep building (or don't, up to you). We split the new revenue 70/30. You keep 70%.

No upfront cost. No selling your app. You keep full ownership. I only make money if you make money.

What I'm looking for

  • Apps with real users (10K+ monthly)
  • Little or no monetization currently
  • Developer who wants help, not a buyer

I'm not a private equity firm. I'm not trying to acquire your app and gut it. I just want to help good apps make money.

Questions

Why would I give up 30%?

30% of something is better than 100% of nothing. Most indie apps make $0. If I can help you make $5K/month, you're keeping $3,500 you didn't have before.

What if I want to sell later?

Your app, your call. The partnership doesn't prevent a sale. If anything, an app making money sells for more.

Will you add annoying ads?

No. I hate that stuff too. Premium features, not ad spam. Your users won't hate you.

How much work is it for me?

As much or as little as you want. Some developers stay involved, some just want the checks. Both are fine.
